Crunchy Cooking Parody

If you've ever watched videos from pretentious creators like Nara Smith, you know there are quite a few stereotypically traditional wives (or "trad" wives) who make it their mission to make you feel bad for not cooking as cleanly or organically as they do. The reality is, their "standards" are unattainable for almost everyone (including themselves!), and their recipes are rarely feasible or even tasty.


This video pokes fun at that mentality and shows that if you really want it, you should have your pie and eat it too -- guilt free!
